### Pricing Psychology Recommendations

1. **Transparent "No Hidden Fees"**: Market as "what you see is what you pay"
2. **Fractional Entry**: "$1 minimum" messaging appeals to accessibility
3. **Fee Comparison Tools**: Show savings vs competitors and physical gold
4. **Loyalty Tiers**: Volume discounts for repeat users (0.08% at $10K+)
5. **Launch Promo**: 0% fees for first month to drive adoption

## Lessons Learned

- Undercutting competitor fees is viable and sustainable
- Storage-free model is a major marketing differentiator
- Premium services diversify revenue without alienating retail users
- Volume-based loyalty program incentivizes whale retention
- "Transparent pricing" messaging resonates with crypto-native users
